Megrim / Medium

Megrim is an experimental font that was designed
by Daniel Johnson. Johnson is a designer at the
Open Font Library of Triad Postnaya. Megrim
visually covers the basics of Western Latin character
set . It is a monoline drawing table sans (Font).
Megrim is featured in more than 45,000 websites and
most popular in the United States and Europe (Specimen).
I was attracted to this font because of its futuristic and
stylish usage of the element of line.
