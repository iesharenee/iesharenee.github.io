Iesha Magallanes

BLOOD.
DAMN. Series

Visual representation of Kendrick Lamar's song "BLOOD."
Acrylic paint and photo collage

I finsinshed off the piece by adding the word "blood".

To hightlight the concept of blood, I took a photograph of a friend's lips and then digitally added a drop of blood to it.

To create a blood affect, I splattered red acrylic paint on a white canvas and then digitally manipulated it by adding blood imagery.

I then created a rough sketch of what my design concept would look like as a whole.

In order to come up with my own visual representation of this song, I played the song about 10 times. As it played, I wrote down the lyrics that stood out the most to me while quickly sketching out relative imagery.
