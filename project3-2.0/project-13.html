Gun Violence Installstion

Gun installation on school shootings and how it has impacted our country.
Digital illustration and typography

We created a circular wire structure that was nailed to a wooden cross that would support all the cermaic multiples and create a circle, allowing viewers to walk in side and around it. I also played sounds of children playing outside to heighten the severity of the issue the installation is trying to tackle.

Our 4D element was an apple which would root as time would go. This symbolized the rotten aspect of school safety when it comes to the protetction of children from guns.

We created 50 gun shell mutiples made out of clay which were also later turned into ceramic. I then splattered red paint on all of them to mimick the effect of blood.

I individually designed the guns by using a scultping knife. These guns were made out of clay and then fired in an oven to turn into firm ceramic.

My team and I began my installation by creating a mold of a gun and then casting 12 guns from it.
