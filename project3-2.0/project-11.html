Skateboard Installation

Installation of 6 skateboards made completely out of wood to represent my journey within the foster care system.
Wood, arcylic paint, and wood stain

Finally, I wood stained a large square piece of wood and glued on the 5 oddly shaped skateboards in the back and placed the replica on the front. The 5 broken skateboards are meant to represent the children damaged within the foster care system while the single golden skateboard represents the .2% of children who make it out and become students at a university or college like myself.

I then created the wooden wheels, bars, and holder elements for my replica skateboard by using a machine saw and a sanding machine. I put all the elements together by using a nail gun and wood glue.

I began the creation of my skateboards by sanding down one 7x28 flat piece of wood with a wood sanding machine. It took about three days to get the actual shape and curvature of a real board. I then made 5 more boards by creating ododities in them by using a drill and a woodhole cutting machine.
