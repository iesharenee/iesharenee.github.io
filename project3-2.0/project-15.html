Sound Wave

The visual conceptualization of a sound wave.
Plastic, wood, vinyl, acrylic paint, and nails

We installed the board onto a wall and added lighting to it to heighten the visual experience.

We then individually hammered down sequences of nails tied with plastic strings onto the board. The idea was that viewers could go up and pluck the plastic strings to conduct a sequence of different noise patterns.

My team and I painted a large wooden board with white acrylic paint. Once it dried, we laid down vinyl letters that would serve as our visual definition of a sound wave.
