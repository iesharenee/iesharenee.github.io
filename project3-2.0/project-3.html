Iesha Magallanes

HUMBLE.
DAMN. Series

Visual representation of Kendrick Lamar's song "LOVE."
Colored Pencils and digital illustration

I completed the piece of by adding a photograph of the Los Angeles skyline and by adding the word "humble" on the bandana.

I digitally created a colorful thought bubble using a bloating tool to symbolize the congreation of idea and expression in the human mind.

I turned a picture of young man into a digital stamp and created a black, dense shape to visualize a bandana over his mouth.

I then created a rough sketch of what my design concept would look like as a whole.

In order to come up with my own visual representation of this song, I played the song about 10 times. As it played, I wrote down the lyrics that stood out the most to me while quickly sketching out relative imagery.
