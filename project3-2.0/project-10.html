The Cactus

Image of a Cactus made out of repeated instances of a paragraph describing a cactus.
Adobe Illustrator

I created a composite image using words describing a cactus by using the orginal image of the cactus as a guide. I adjsuted and manipulated the lighting and contrast of the image to mark areas where I would place signficantly colored paragpraphs. These colored paragraphs would come together to mimick the actual pattern and surface of the cactus as realstic as possible.

A photo that I took of a cactus stem in my backyard in Southern California.
