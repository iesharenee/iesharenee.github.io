LUST.
DAMN. Series

Visual representation of Kendrick Lamar's song "LUST."
Adobe Photoshop snd Adobe Photoshop

I placed the dollar bill background behind the cut out image of the lips then put the word "lust" on the tongue candy.

I digitally manipulated a dollar bill with color and splatter effects. I then created two more copies of the dollar bill and put them in a liner format to create my background.

I made a cut out of the lips from this image to use as the lustful mouth in my deisgn.

I then created a rough sketch of what my design concept would look like as a whole.

In order to come up with my own visual representation of this song, I played the song about 10 times. As it played, I wrote down the lyrics that stood out the most to me while quickly sketching out relative imagery.
