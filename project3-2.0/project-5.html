Iesha Magallanes

LOYALTY.
DAMN. Series

Visual representation of Kendrick Lamar's song "LOYALTY."
Pencil and photo collage

I finished off the piece by putiing all the elements together and by adding the word "loyalty" at the bottom.

I digitally created an image of a heart using the bloating tool and then put it against a marble background.

I sketched an image of two hands clasping one another with pencil and then manipulated the colors levels digitally.

I then created a rough sketch of what my design concept would look like as a whole.

In order to come up with my own visual representation of this song, I played the song about 10 times. As it played, I wrote down the lyrics that stood out the most to me while quickly sketching out relative imagery.
