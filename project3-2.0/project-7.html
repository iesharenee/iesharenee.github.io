PRIDE.
DAMN. Series

Visual representation of Kendrick Lamar's song "PRIDE."
Digital illustration and photo collage

I placed the cut out behind a colorful painte background and added the word "pride" at the bottom.

I digitally color manipulated a cut out of two cuffed hands to highlight the power witin the diversity of the LGBTQ community.

I then created a rough sketch of what my design concept would look like as a whole.

In order to come up with my own visual representation of this song, I played the song about 10 times. As it played, I wrote down the lyrics that stood out the most to me while quickly sketching out relative imagery.
