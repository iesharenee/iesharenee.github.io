Iesha Magallanes

GOD.
DAMN. Series

Visual representation of Kendrick Lamar's song "GOD."
Digital illustration and photo collage

I filled the lion with color and added grass underneath it. I then put the lion in front of the first page of the book of Genesis in the bible while highlighting the word "God" in purple.

I took a picture of a lion and turned it into a digital sketch by configuring shapes and eccentric patterns throughout its body.

I then created a rough sketch of what my design concept would look like as a whole.

In order to come up with my own visual representation of this song, I played the song about 10 times. As it played, I wrote down the lyrics that stood out the most to me while quickly sketching out relative imagery.
