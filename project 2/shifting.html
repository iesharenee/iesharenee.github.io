The clutch pedal is what makes driving a manual car more difficult
than an automatic car. Without going into too much detail, the clutch
is essentially two metal plates that connect the engine to the drive wheels. So, by pressing down on the clutch pedal, you're disconnecting the engine from the wheels.

The gas pedal serves the same fucntion as it does in an automatic car however, to move in any direction, you have to slowly release the gas and press your right foot on the clutch simultaneously.
